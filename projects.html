Besign

2nd Place · NTP13 2026 Best Entrepreneurial Project

An AI-powered custom tailoring platform connecting customers with tailors and fabric stores in Amman. My graduation project — awarded Best Entrepreneurial Project against a field of more than 100, and second place at NTP13 2026.

Problem: Getting clothes made runs on guesswork. Customers cannot picture the result before committing, measurements are taken by hand and vary from tailor to tailor, and there is no way to check what arrived against what was promised.

Solution: A Flutter marketplace covering the whole loop: a customer app, a tailor studio, and a shared design system. Its body-measurement feature runs a YOLOv8-pose model on the phone, turning a single photo into 12 measurements with no server round-trip. AI features sit behind a provider-neutral gateway, so the model can be swapped without touching app code.

VocalEyes

Prototype

An assistive companion app for smart glasses, built for people with visual impairments and the caregivers who support them.

Problem: People with visual impairments depend on someone else for everyday visual decisions, such as what they are wearing or what is in front of them. Their caregivers, meanwhile, have no straightforward way to stay in the loop.

Solution: A Flutter app built around two distinct roles. The user side handles pairing with the glasses, outfit assistance, and scan history; the caregiver side connects to a user and reviews their scans and location. I designed the role-based navigation and an accessibility-first interface.

Flutter Dart Supabase Accessibility

Honest status: a working prototype. The device and caregiver services run on mocked data while the glasses hardware integration is still pending.

Glamé AI

Design & Specification

An AI makeup companion app. This one is not code — it is the full product specification and system design behind it.

Problem: Beauty apps recommend what they want to sell you. They ignore the products already sitting in your makeup bag, and they ignore what your face actually needs.

Solution: As the software engineer on a two-person team, I authored the complete specification for an owned-first product: an AI architecture where facial-feature analysis, adaptation, and recommendation engines sit behind a provider-neutral inference gateway; a phased roadmap from MVP to Pro; a requirements traceability matrix; and the full UML set — class, architecture, ER, sequence, use case, activity, and deployment diagrams.

Software Specification UML / PlantUML System Architecture Flutter Supabase

Honest status: design and specification work. The app itself is not built; an early Flutter prototype is archived.
